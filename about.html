About me

Hey, my name is Kaelan Foley. I was born in Redwood City and lived there for most of my childhood. If you had asked me in high school what I wanted to do in life, I'd honestly say I didn't expect to be around this long. And as dark as that is, it still rings true to this day. So I've spent the last few years trying to figure that out. And while I've bounced around with multiple interests and potential carrer paths, there has always been a creative side to my interests. Whether it be music, film, photography, or now writing.
